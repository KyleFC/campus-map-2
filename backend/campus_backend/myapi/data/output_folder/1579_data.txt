Center for Career & Vocation
Third Year | Professional Development

As a third-year student, the goal is to gain professional experience in areas internships, part-time jobs. Now is a great time to get a part-time job, internship, or develop professional skills and gain resume-building experience. Below is a checklist of steps to take towards continuing your 4 Year Career Roadmap.

Review your career goals and check-in with how you are working towards those goals.
Build your network by connecting with professors, mentors, coaches, peers, bosses, and personal contacts.
Join CUI Connect and find an alumni mentor.
Review your Handshake profile and social media presence. Determine the personal brand that you want to portray.
Upload an updated resume to Handshake and apply for a part-time job or internship through Handshake.
Explore graduate schools and programs.
Attend the Fall Grad Fair.
Meet with a career coach to help with mock interviews, resume reviews, and more.
Follow us on social media.
Attend Workshops + Events
Career Readiness Competencies to Work On:
Career Management | Professionalism + Work Ethic | Digital Technology
