The Caf
Dining Commons

Concordia University Irvine offers an array of food choices and services for students. Bon Appetit, Concordia University Irvine's premiere caterer has won many awards in the food service industry for their healthy food options and home cooked favorites. They provide all the food in the main cafeteria and Eagles' Landing. They also prepare all of our on- and off-campus catering needs.
