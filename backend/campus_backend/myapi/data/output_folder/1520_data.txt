Required and Important Dates

All student leaders will be expected to be available for the following dates:

Register and attend Leadership Development INT 105 classes in Fall 2022 or Spring 2023 semester.
Attend Spring 2022 Leadership Training on March 18.
Return to campus for fall student leadership training during the first 2 weeks of August and participate in SOAR (August 7- August 21). This will be a full time commitment during these dates. No exceptions.
Return to campus for spring student leadership training in January 2023 and participate in Spring Orientation.
Questions
