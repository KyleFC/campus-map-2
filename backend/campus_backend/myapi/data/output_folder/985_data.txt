GRADUATE FINANCIAL AID
Scholarships

A scholarship is a type of financial aid that does not need to be repaid. Scholarships are a form of gift aid that is typically based on merit, such as, academic excellence, talent, affiliation with various groups, or career aspirations or a combination of merit and need.

Scholarships for Graduate/Adult Students

There are a variety of resources available to assist you as a graduate student. Concordia scholarship opportunities are listed by program:

Cross-cultural Ministry Center (CMC) Scholarships
Available to students in the MA Theology: Cross-Cultural Ministry (CMC) program only
Church Career Scholarship
Available to students in the MA Theology program with a Director of Christian Education (DCE) emphasis only
MCAA/MCSE Scholarship
Scholarship Athletes, Graduate Assistants, and students receiving a Concordia Faculty/Staff Waiver cannot combine those with tuition discounts, including the MCAA/MCSE scholarship.
Golden State Teacher Grant
Available only to students in the MAED School Counseling (with pupil personnel services credential) and M.Ed with preliminary credential programs.
