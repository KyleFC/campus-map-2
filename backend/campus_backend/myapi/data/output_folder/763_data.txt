Things to Do Nearby

 

Located in Southern California, Orange County is an area brimming with entertainment, shopping and sightseeing options. Concordia is just six miles from the ocean, and Disneyland is so close you can see the fireworks every night.

Here are just a few of the many entertainment and cultural options:

Irvine Spectrum Shopping Mall: 3.6 miles
Balboa Island: 7.5 miles
Segerstrom Center for the Arts: 4.7 miles
Newport Beach: 6 miles
Disneyland: 16 miles
Knott’s Berry Farm: 22 miles
Los Angeles : 43 miles
Universal Studios: 51 miles
Big Bear Ski Resort: 96 miles
