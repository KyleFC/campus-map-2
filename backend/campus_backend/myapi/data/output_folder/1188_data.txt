Phone-A-Thon
CONCORDIA serves

Join us as we make phone calls to shut-ins. We will provide you with a simple script and talking points. Bring a little light to a shut-in's day!

 Tuesday, September 26, 2023, 10:00am - 12:00pm

 Concordia University Irvine - Alpha Building

Please bring your cell phone. You will use it to make the calls. We will show you how to mask your number when calling.
