Getting Started

#1

Access MyCUI

Bookmark my.cui.edu in your web browser and download the MyCUI app in the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android).

#2

Login @ MyCUI

Next, login to MyCUI at my.cui.edu or in the app using you E number and password. This is the same password as you use for your CUI email account.

#3

Update Profile

After logging in the first thing you'll want to do is update your profile and account settings.

#4

Discover MyCUI

Navigate the main menu and existing widgets to explore the many features and tools immediately available. Use the global search to discover groups and pages that interest you.

#5

Watch the MyCUI Video Tutorials

Take advantage of the video tutorial library to learn more about the tools and features available in MyCUI.
