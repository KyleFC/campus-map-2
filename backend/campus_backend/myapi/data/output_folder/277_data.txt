Purpose of Church and Community Relations
Develop and maintain relationships with churches and schools.
Help Concordia University to be more visible in the Christian community at large.
Assist in recruiting students from Lutheran and other Christian schools as well as churches.
Raise scholarship support for students pursuing careers in full-time church work professions.
Promote and nurture Christian leadership development.
Church and Community Relations Services
Coordinate visits of Concordia's student music and theatre ministry teams. View the Performing Arts Outreach Calendar.
Assist faculty with speaking and preaching engagements at churches.
Church Relations staff are available to churches for Bible studies, guest preaching, and pulpit replacements.
