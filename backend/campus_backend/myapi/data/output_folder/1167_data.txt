Giving
Visual Arts Department

DONATE TODAY

The Art Patrons support the visual arts at Concordia through the sponsorship of scholarships, purchasing tools and equipment, defraying student expenses for shows and exhibits, and supporting the collection of the John and Linda Friend Gallery in Grimm Hall. Listed below are the benefits for each level of giving:

Annual Giving Levels

Loyalty Circle: $1,000 - $2,499 *

Pacesetter Dinner, Pre-sale window for Christmas Concert, a copy of the Aerie+, Patron Reception invitation, CUI decal, program listing

Director's Circle: $500 - $999

A copy of the Aerie+, Patron Reception invitation, CUI decal, program listing

Ambassador’s Circle: $250 - 499

Patron Reception invitation, CUI decal, program listing

Partner’s Circle: $100 - $249

CUI Decal, program listing

Friend’s Circle: $25 - 99

Program listing

The tax-deductible portion of your contribution will be reflected on your gift receipt.

DONATE TODAY

*Patrons at The Art Masters and above are eligible for additional benefits through Pacesetters, Concordia University’s leadership giving society.

+The Aerie is Concordia’s Literary & Art Publication; an annual journal that showcases work being done in creative writing and art by Concordia University Irvine students, alumni, faculty, and staff.
