SCHOOL OF BUSINESS AND ECONOMICS DEAN'S ADVISORY BOARD

The Dean's Advisory Board (DAB) for the School of Business and Economics is a "sounding board" for the discussion of advanced academic initiatives and university strategies to continue providing employers with business graduates who hold the highest academic and ethical standards.
