CAMPUS SAFETY
CSA Report Form

The Clery Act requires all Campus Security Authorities (CSA) to immediately report crimes to the Department of Campus Safety as soon as they are made aware of them. The contents of this form will be sent to the Director and Associate Director of Campus Safety for review. For accuracy of reporting Clery crime statistics, please fill out this form completely. Any questions regarding the form or CSA reporting requirements should be directed to:

Director of Campus Safety
Raul Morales
(949)214-3007
raul.morales@cui.edu

Associate Director of Campus Safety
Steven Rodriguez
(949)214-3003
steven.rodriguez@cui.edu

This form should not be used to report crimes in progress or incidents that pose an immediate threat to the larger community. Reports submitted through this web form may not receive an immediate response. If a crime requires an immediate response, please call 9-1-1 and then Campus Safety Dispatch at 949-214-3000. For other immediate assistance, please contact Campus Safety Dispatch. This form is only for faculty, staff, and student employees who are considered Campus Security Authorities. Persons who are not employed by Concordia University Irvine who would like to report a crime can fill out the Silent Witness form or call Campus Safety Dispatch
