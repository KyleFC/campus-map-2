OUR HONORS PROGRAM WILL PUSH YOU
 Apply Now  Visit Concordia  Talk to a Counselor  Request Info  Virtual Tour  Deposit

If you're ready to reach deeper, farther and higher in your education, our Honors Program will challenge you. Honors students enjoy a variety of Honors course options, smaller class sizes, and special opportunities for those planning to attend graduate school. Freshmen students are invited to join based on your high school GPA and standardized test scores.

Honors Opportunities
Take Honors sections of courses for general education and major requirements
Study for a semester at Concordia's Cambridge Program
Explore an intellectual interest with a professor in an Honors Investigation
Present your research at an undergraduate conference
Graduate with an Honors distinction
Develop strong habits of scholarship for a life of service
Receive a merit scholarship, the Honors Scholarship, or the Presidential Honors Scholarship
