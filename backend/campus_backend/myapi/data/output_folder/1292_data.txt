Concordia Connect: A New Homecoming

Everything you've loved about Homecoming...and then some! Join fellow alumni back on-campus for an event unlike any other. The campus will come alive with faculty and student representatives from all departments on the Commons Lawn. Starting at 2:00 pm with music, a devotion, and campus updates from President Thomas. Then, connect with former professors, current students, and fellow alumni in an open-house style gathering on the Commons Lawn and finish the day with an exclusive alumni happy hour social.
