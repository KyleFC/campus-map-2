PPOHA
We are excited to announce the launch of our Graduate Resource Center. This resource will provide extra writing and success coaching for each of our students in the School of Education. Over the next few months, we will roll out more resources to you, all with the goal of supporting your personal, academic, and program success.

Services offered in the Graduate Resource Center:
Referrals to University and campus resources including financial aid, health and wellness, counseling, and career support
Individual appointments focused on personal, spiritual, social, career development
Mock Interviews- as you think about moving to the next level of your career, we are here to support your next steps
Resume development
Personal and academic coaching
Writing support
General support for navigating your program

Our Graduate Resource Center is funded by the Promoting Postbaccalaureate Opportunities for Hispanic Americans (PPOHA) Grant.
To learn more about the grant, please look here: Concordia University Irvine PPOHA Grant

We are also excited to announce scholarships available to post-baccalaureate, M.A. and Ed.D. students. Our Future Faculty Fellows scholarships are for underserved students with a financial need. There are income limits for this scholarship, but if you are eligible, we strongly encourage you to apply. More information can be found here: Future Faculty Fellows Program and Eligibility Requirements.

Our Graduate Resource Center is led by Jamie Briner and Tim Young. You can find their bios here: Ed.d. & M.A. Faculty
