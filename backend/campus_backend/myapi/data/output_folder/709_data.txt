ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
Master of Science in Nursing: Family Nurse Practitioner (MSN-FNP)
Admission Requirements
Submit a graduate application for admission.
Payment of a nonrefundable application fee.
Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) degree from a regionally accredited institution with a minimum GPA of 3.0 (on a 4.0 scale).
Completion of nursing research, statistics, health assessment, and pathophysiology with a grade of C or better.
A copy ofÂ current unrestricted registered nursing license from California.
Submit a written 2-page statement of intent (please refer to the essay prompt below).
Submit a resume showing recent registered nursing experience.
Provide a minimum of 2 professional letters of recommendation.
Signed Background Check Waiver form.
