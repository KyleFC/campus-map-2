Educator Leadership Academy
 
Who

K-12 public and private school education professionals.

Schools and District Offices are encouraged to send at least three team members from their site
Enrollment will be limited to 100 participants

Register Today ‌

What

Concordia University’s Servant Leadership Institute is sponsoring five days of professional development for educators across the nation to acquire insights on how to become exemplary servant leaders of character with specific skills to create positive school cultures where students thrive.

View Details ‌

When

The Educator Leadership Academy begins with three days of professional development training with seminar sessions, networking with colleagues, and time to meet with leadership coaches.

Thursday, May 16, 2024 (4:30 pm - 8:00 pm)
Friday, May 17, 2024 (8:00 am - 7:00 pm)
Saturday, May 18, 2024 (8:00 am - 2:15 pm)

The Academy reconvenes in February with follow-up debriefing and additional leadership seminars.

Friday, February 7, 2025 (8:00 am - 4:00 pm)
Saturday, February 8, 2025 (8:00 am - 2:30 pm)

Leadership Coaching

As an added benefit of the Educator Leadership Academy, each school team will be matched with a trained Leadership Coach who will meet via Zoom for 60-90 minutes/month through the academic year.
