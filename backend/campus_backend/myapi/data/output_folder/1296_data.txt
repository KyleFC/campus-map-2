PRESIDENT'S ADVISORY COUNCIL
2022-2023

The President's Advisory Council (PAC) is a group of senior-level business, education, and community leaders who advise University President Dr. Michael Thomas in furthering Concordia University Irvine's strategic position as an educational leader in Southern California.
