Concordia In Touch

Concordia In Touch is an internal email that is sent to all faculty and staff, including adjuncts, every Monday and Thursday.

Submission deadlines:

To be included in the Monday newsletter, information must be submitted by Friday prior to 12 p.m.
To be included in the Thursday newsletter, information must be submitted by Wednesday prior to 12 p.m.

There is no guarantee that your submission will appear in the Concordia In Touch. All submissions will be approved at the discretion of the Office of Strategic Communications. All event submissions must be University sponsored or approved. News and event entries may run in multiple issues, at the discretion of the Office of Strategic Communications. Submissions should not include grammatical or spelling errors.

To enter a submission, please complete this form.

If you have any questions, please email cuiintouch@cui.edu.
