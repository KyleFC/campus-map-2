Giving
Theatre Department

DONATE TODAY

Theatre Patrons provide gifts to the University's theatre department. The performing arts have been an integral part of the Concordia experience since the school's earliest days and the theatre programs have grown to require three fully-equipped performance spaces: the CU Center, the Studio Theatre, and the Nelson Outdoor Amphitheatre. Listed below are the benefits for each level of giving:

Annual Giving Levels

Virtuoso’s Circle: $10,000+

VIP Events throughout the year, Christmas Reception with President, Two tickets to Christmas Concert, Pre-sale window for Christmas Concert, Pacesetter Dinner, Patron reception invitation, Four tickets to campus events, CUI Decal, Program Listing

Executive Producer’s Circle: $5,000 to $9,999

Christmas Reception with President, Two tickets to Christmas, Pre-sale window to Christmas Concert, Pacesetter Dinner, Patron reception invitation, Four tickets to campus events, CUI Decal, Program Listing

Playwright’s Circle: $2,500 to $4,999

Two tickets to Christmas, Pre-sale window for Christmas Concert, Pacesetter Dinner, Patron reception invitation, Two tickets to campus events, CUI Decal, Program Listing

Loyalty Circle: $1,000-$2,499

Pre-sale window for Christmas Concert, Pacesetter Dinner, Patron reception invitation, Two tickets to campus events, CUI Decal, Program Listing

Director’s Circle: $500-$999

Two tickets to campus events, CUI Decal, Program Listing

Ambassador’s Circle: $250-$499

CUI Decal, program listing

Partner’s Circle: $100-$249

CUI Decal, program listing

Friend’s Circle: $25-$99

Program listing

The tax-deductible portion of your contribution will be reflected on your gift receipt.

DONATE TODAY

Patrons at the Loyalty Circle and above are eligible for additional benefits through Pacesetters, Concordia University’s leadership giving society.
