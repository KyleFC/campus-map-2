Estate Planning
New to Estate Planning?

Just getting started and want to know where to begin? Planning your estate may seem daunting, but it's much easier than you think. The hardest step to take is the first one. Reach out to a member of Concordia University Irvine's Planned Giving Team. We are here to walk you through the process step by step AT NO COST. You can email us at plannedgiving@cui.edu or call 949-214-3535 to connect with a representative.
