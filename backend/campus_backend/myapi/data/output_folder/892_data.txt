How to Apply
LIVING LEARNING COMMUNITIES
Applying for a Living Learning Community (LLC)
It is important to submit your application as soon as possible, there are a limited number of available spots in each LLC.
Applications are first-come, first-served, and will be received by the LLC application committee in that order.
Before choosing the community you would like to apply to, be sure to read the LLC description clearly. 
LLCs will accept any interested student with a sophomore, junior, or senior class standing. Transfer students are welcome.
How to Apply
Complete your housing application at cui.erezlife.com
Select which Living Learning Community (LLC) you would like to apply to
Please note the following building locations for each LLC:
Kappa - Polis Plaza
Theta - The Creative Edge
Lambda - Global Village
Omicron - Bella Amore

Once you have completed your housing application, you will receive an email regarding your application status from cui.erezlife.com. If you receive an email with a different building assignment, the LLC’s may be full and you can sign-up to live in another residence hall. Email res@cui.edu with any questions.
