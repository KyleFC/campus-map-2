Coursework

In addition to your credential coursework, the following religious education courses will be completed to attain your Lutheran Teacher Certificate:

Thl 201: History & Literature of the Old Testament (3) also a general ed requirement
Thl 202: History & Literature of the New Testament (3) also a general ed requirement
Thl 371: Christian Doctrine I (3)
Thl 372: Christian Doctrine II (3)
Thl 304: History of the Christian Church (3)
CCI 103: Introduction to Ministry (1)
CCI 305: Called to Teach the Faith (3)
CCI 113: Seminar in Ministry (.5) Enrollment in this course is required every spring semester to retain the CCI grant or Church Vocations Scholarship.
