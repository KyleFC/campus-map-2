The art of actively getting to know people

Connect with family, friends, church members, Concordia's faculty and staff, visitors at career events
Contacts from volunteer, work, and internship experiences
Joining a professional association in your field
Use social networking for the job search
Be prepared to quickly introduce yourself with a clear and concise elevator pitch
