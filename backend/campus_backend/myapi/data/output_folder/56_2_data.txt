DCE Internship

A unique component of the DCE minor is a paid internship during your final year in the program. Students will spend 12 months on-site with a serving DCE to acquire experience and skills related to DCE ministry, including a study of current DCE ministry trends, resources, and philosophies. Your fieldwork is intended to feel like a home-away-from-home experience, while at the same time challenging your predispositions or assumptions so you can grow in wisdom and faith.
