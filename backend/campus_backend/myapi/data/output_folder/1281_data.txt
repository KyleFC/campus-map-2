THRIVENT CHOICE
Giving at Concordia University Irvine

Eligible members can recommend where Thrivent distributes a portion of its charitable funding by directing Choice Dollars® to Concordia University Irvine. Members (age 16 or older) are eligible if they meet one or more of the following criteria:

Have $800 or more in annual premiums of qualifying insurance products.
Have $20,000 or more in contract value on qualifying products.
Hold a qualifying Thrivent Financial volunteer leadership position.

For more information about this program and how to direct your dollars to Concordia, visit www.thrivent.com/thriventchoice.

For sharing this information about Thrivent Choice, our Thrivent Member Network is giving us a chance to win a donation of up to $1,000 in April 2023!
