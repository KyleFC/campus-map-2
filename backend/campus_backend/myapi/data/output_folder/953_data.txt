UNDERGRADUATE FINANCIAL AID

We encourage you to schedule a financial aid appointment to discuss your anticipated charges, financial aid award and payment options. The appointment will last approximately 30 minutes and family members are strongly encouraged to participate in the appointment.

In order to schedule a financial aid appointment, you must be accepted to Concordia University Irvine and have received a financial aid package. If you have not yet been accepted or have not received your financial aid award, please contact your Admissions Counselor or email visitsandevents@cui.edu

An admissions staff member will contact you within 48 hours to schedule your appointment upon submitting the form below.
