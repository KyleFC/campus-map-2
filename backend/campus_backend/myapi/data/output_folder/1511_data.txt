MyRecords Login
MyRecords is the central location for all of your personal records at Concordia, including:
Viewing and accepting your Financial Aid
Viewing and paying your student bill
Viewing or changing your personal information
To log into MyRecords do the following:
Go to https://www.cui.edu/mycui and click the MyRecords link.
Use your CUI online resources user ID as your "User ID".
Make sure to use a capital "E" and all of the leading zeros in your User ID (ex. E00654321).
Enter your MyRecords password, which is the same as your CUI email.
