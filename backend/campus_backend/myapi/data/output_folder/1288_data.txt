FRIENDS OF CONCORDIA
A Proud Tradition of Support and Service to Students

The Friends of Concordia raises unrestricted annual funds for the University. These gifts compliment funds from other sources. Friends of Concordia members help Concordia achieve its goal of assuming its place as one of the nation's preeminent Christian universities. Gifts are used to support a variety of needs on campus.

There are a number of ways you can give to Concordia University and become a Friend of Concordia member:

Friends of Concordia - Founders Donor
Donors contribute at least $500 but less than $1,000 in unrestricted gifts to the University annually.

Friends of Concordia - Good Shepherd Donor
Donors contribute at least $250 but less than $500 in unrestricted gifts to the University annually.

Friends of Concordia - Gold
Donors contribute at least $100 but less than $249 in unrestricted gifts to the University annually.

Friends of Concordia - Green
Donors contribute less than $100 in unrestricted gifts to the University annually.

You are invited to become a Friend of Concordia and join in Concordia's commitment to faithfulness and excellence. For more information, please contact Bil Hood at (949) 214-3189 or bil.hood@cui.edu. To maximize the impact of your gift to Concordia, check if you or your spouse qualify for a matching gift program.
