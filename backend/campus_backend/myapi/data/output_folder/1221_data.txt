Handshake

Handshake is Concordia Irvine’s online job and internship career hub for our students and alumni. Employers can use Handshake to post jobs and internships, as well as register for recruiting events such as Career Fairs, Summer Camp and Ministry Fairs, and Employer Panels.

LOGIN TO HANDSHAKE

Create a free account or log in to Handshake above. Then click “post a job” button to get started.

Travel + Directions

Driving Directions - From North

Take I-405 S toward San Diego
Take University Dr. exit - Exit 4
Turn right onto University Dr.
Turn left onto Ridgeline Dr.
Turn right onto Concordia East (Gate Access Required)

Driving Directions - From South

Take I-5 N toward Los Angeles
Merge onto I-405 N toward Long Beach
Take Jeffery Rd. exit - Exit 4
Turn left onto Jeffery Rd. (Jeffery Rd. will turn into University Dr.)
Turn left onto Ridgeline Dr.
Turn right onto Concordia East (Gate Access Required)
