EDP 593D: Field Experience III (D)
3

This course yields supervised field experience leading to competencies in the School Psychology Performance Expectations. Candidates will gain and document four hundred (400) clock hours of field experience toward the twelve hundred (1200) requirement in accordance with credential parameters and with satisfactory evaluations by university and on-site supervisors. Prerequisite: EDP 592D (concurrent enrollment allowed). 

CAPSTONE EXPERIENCE
EDP 580: Educational Research
3

This course will provide students with an examination of the field of educational research by exploring qualitative research models, analyzing research articles, and applying measurement and basic statistical techniques to problems or issues in education. Students will learn the essentials needed to carry out the entire research process and develop a  collaborative research proposal that will guide the qualitative action research project.

EDP 605: Capstone
3

During this course, students work to finalize their capstone project. Students will triangulate data using critical self reflections, credible literature, and observations as a method to respond to an educational problem. Upon completion of the written project, students will orally present their capstone in class.
