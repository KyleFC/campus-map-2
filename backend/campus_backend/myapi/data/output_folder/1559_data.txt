What is Available at Concordia?

Desktop computers available for student use at various locations including the Library, Grimm Hall Mac Lab, and Grimm Hall PC Lab. Review FAQs regarding campus computers and Library computer use code of conduct prior to use.

Microsoft Office 365 Standard

Concordia students are now eligible for a free copy of Microsoft Office 365 Standard. On this getting started page, enter your student email address (firstname.lastname@eagles.cui.edu) and follow the instructions provided. You will likely be asked to go through two-factor authentication. You can find assistance via this guide: Microsoft Office 2-Factor Authentication.

Software Discounts Through AICCU

Concordia has developed a partnership with journeyED.com to provide enrolled students with software at an affordable price. Click on the link and select "Concordia University of California" to visit Concordia's personalized online store.

Concordia Apple Discount Store

Apple offers special pricing on the full range of Macintosh computers plus select third-party products. Click on the link to gain access to the Higher Education online store.
