Wellness Center

Concordia University Irvineâ€™s Wellness Center is dedicated to providing high quality medical and psychological care to our students in a private, confidential, and safe setting. The staff is committed to the wellness of our students.

Patient Hours & Location

The Wellness Center is located on the first floor of the Grimm Student Union building by the mailroom.

Fall & Spring Semesters

Monday - Friday: 9:00 am - 4:00 pm

Summer Break

Monday - Thursday: 9:00 am - 12:00 pm and 1:00 pm - 4:00 pm
Friday: 9:00 am - 12:00 pm

Stop by, call, or email to make an appointment. If we are away from our desk, please leave a message with your callback number. Messages are regularly checked during normal business hours. We are closed on weekends and holidays.

(949) 214-3102
wellness@cui.edu

After Hours

The Wellness Center is not able to accommodate emergency or after-hours services. For health care needs after hours, you can call the Nurse Helpline on your insurance card, if available, or go to the nearest hospital or urgent care. If you have no medical insurance, go to the nearest hospital. Concordia University Irvine is not liable for medical expenses incurred.

For emergency, please call 911 or go to the nearest hospital.

If this is a mental health crisis or urgent concern, please call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
