Scholarship

Concordia University Irvine is proud to offer a $1,000 scholarship to all new students when you begin the MCAA program. More details are available through your admissions counselor. Get started by requesting information or starting your application.

Note: Scholarship Athletes, Graduate Assistants, and students receiving a Concordia Faculty/Staff Waiver are not eligible for the $1,000 scholarship.
