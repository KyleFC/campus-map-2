VETERANS RESOURCE CENTER
GIVE NOW

There are two ways that you can give towards the Staff Sergeant Matthew Thompson Veterans Resource Center!

Endowed Scholarship. Concordia University Irvine began receiving funding toward an endowed scholarship for veterans in 2017. The title of the scholarship "The Armed Services Award" contains the acronym "SERVICES", which stands for Serving Every Veteran In College Endowed Scholarship.

Current Programs. A donation to our current programs will help foster community and increase the graduation rates of our veterans by supporting them with programming events, textbooks for our lending library, coffee, snacks, and other items.

Checks can be sent as follows:
Payable to: Concordia University Irvine
Memo Line: "Endowed Scholarship" or "Current Programs"

Address:
University Advancement
1530 Concordia West
Irvine, CA 92612-9989
