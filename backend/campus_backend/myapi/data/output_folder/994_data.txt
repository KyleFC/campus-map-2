Housing Policy

All traditional undergraduate students are required to live in on-campus housing unless they qualify for one of the following exceptions:

Living at home with their parents or legal guardian
21 years of age before the first day of classes
Is married or is getting married before the start of the semester
Are a parent or primary care-giver to their child
Carrying fewer than 9 units of university course work

All students must fill out aÂ Housing Application Form (students who wish to appeal must complete the Housing Appeals Form - Due June 1st), every year by the following dates:

June 1st for all returning traditional undergraduate students
August 1st for all new traditional undergraduate students
September 1st for all late admits

Students who fail to do so will be charged an application late fee of $250 and the applicable room and board rate (applied to their student account) until they come into compliance with the Housing Policy by either moving on campus, qualifying for an exemption (listed above), or being granted approval by the Off-campus Housing Appeals Committee.
