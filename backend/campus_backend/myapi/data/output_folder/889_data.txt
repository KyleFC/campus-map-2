Faculty in Residence

Dr. Christian Hauser is Associate Professor of Music at Concordia University Irvine. He completed his Ph.D. in music education from the University of North Texas received his undergraduate degree in Commercial Music from Belmont University in Nashville, Tennessee. Dr. Hauser is a strong advocate for self-expression through the arts. He is a composer, performer, and songwriter. He (along with his talented wife) have written, directed and produced several children's musicals. He leads worship at Light of Christ Lutheran Church, playing organ, piano, ukulele, guitar and cajon. He loves every and any outdoor activity, especially tennis, disc golf, ultimate frisbee, football, camping, hiking, snorkeling and skiing.

Dr. Christian Hauser
