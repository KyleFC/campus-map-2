Q&A with Megan

Where are you from?
Huntington Beach, California

What did you study in college?
I attended Concordia University Irvine and earned my Undergraduate Degree with Exercise and Sport Science. I then also received my Master's Degree from Concordia University Irvine in Coaching and Athletic Administration.

What is your favorite place to eat in Irvine?
Eureka!

What do you like to do with your free time?
I love to travel and see new places.

What advice do you have for students going through the admissions process?
Ask questions, visit the campus as much as you want! This is such a fun and exciting time in your life. You want to make sure where you go feels like home.
