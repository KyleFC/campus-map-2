Be a catalyst for change.

The Townsend Institute's graduate certificate program for Professional Executive Coaching Certificate at Concordia University provides intentional and engaging coursework to grow your coaching toolbox. Guided by Dr. John Townsend, the Institute Fellows, and our experienced faculty, students learn how to partner with their clients in a thought-provoking, creative, and collaborative process that inspires them to maximize their personal and professional potential.

Accessible: Online Classes
Offered in English
Affordable: $635 Cost Per Unit
Accredited: ICF Approved Coach Training Program
Accredited: WASC Senior College and University Commission Accredited
Accelerated: 8 Month Program
Start in February or August
Learn how to facilitate transformational relationships
Become a practical, skills-based practitioner
Personally learn from Dr. John Townsend
Engage with world-class Christian thought leaders
