Lutheran Teacher Certificate Requirements
Be a member of a congregation of The Lutheran Church-Missouri Synod (LCMS)  
Complete the prescribed elementary or secondary subject matter preparation program  
Complete religious education coursework with a minimum grade of C- in each course  
Complete the Multiple Subject (elementary) or Single Subject Credential (high school) coursework  
Receive the recommendation of the Concordia University faculty  
Complete ½ of the Student Teaching semester in a regionally accredited school affiliated with the LCMS  
File with the State of California for a teacher credential
