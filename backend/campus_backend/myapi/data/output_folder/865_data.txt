Location and Hours
WRITING STUDIO
Location

The Writing studio is located on the top floor of the Schroeder Library and Learning Center.

Hours

Hours vary each semester. You'll see the current hours and available appointment times when you log in to WCOnline.

Summer Hours

During the summer, the Writing Studio offers online services only. Submit your paper using the Online Writing Lab.

Regular Hours

We are closed for Chapel from 10:30-11:00 on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, and on designated holidays. The Writing Studio takes walk-ins, but appointments are recommended. Make your appointment by going to our online system, WCONLINE.

Appointments are generally 45 minutes; however, longer appointments are possible based on availability.
