Welcome to the Golden Eagle Family!

We are so excited that you have chosen Concordia University Irvine. The Office of Orientation and First-Year Experience (FYE) is here to provide you with the resources to ensure a successful transition to Concordia. Our office is available to help you navigate your first year, whether as a new freshman or transfer student, and help make Concordia your new home.
