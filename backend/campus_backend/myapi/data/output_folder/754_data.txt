Questions About Your Bill

The Bursar's Office at Concordia University finalizes and mails student bills each month. What actually appears on the bill is determined by other departments. Should you have a question about your bill, please refer to the list below and contact the appropriate department.

The main number for Concordia University is (949) 854-8002.

Contact Numbers
Charge/Credit relates to:	Office to Contact	Campus Extension
Parking Fines	Campus Safety & Security	1101
Housing Fines	Residence Life	3044
Health Insurance	Health Center	3102
Housing Assignments	Residence Life	3044
Meal Plan Assignments	Residence Life	3044
Library Fines	Library	librarian@cui.edu
Music Scholarships or Private Lessons	Music Department	3419
Tuition, Late Payment, Instructor Fees	Bursar's Office	3075
Class, Late Registration, Graduation Fees	Registrar	3079

Payments, Payroll Deductions	Bursar's Office	3075
Disputes About Your Bill

Disputes about your bill must be submitted in writing (email is fine). Make sure to include your full name and student ID number. You may mail your dispute to Concordia University, Bursar's Office, 1530 Concordia West, Irvine, CA 92612. The University will answer any disputes in writing within 30 days.
