CAMPUS SAFETY
Campus Safety Office - Admin 103

The Campus Safety Office is located in the Administration Building Suite 103. Campus Safety officers are on duty 24/7. If no one is present in the office, please contact Campus Safety Dispatch at 949-214-3000 or extension 3000 if dialing from an on-campus phone. Student Safety Assistants provide all routine services such as parking decals, ID cards, key requests, lost and found, certified driver applications, and citation appeals at this location.

Office Hours

Monday through Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Closed on weekends and holidays.
