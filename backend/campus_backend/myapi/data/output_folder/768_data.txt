CAMPUS SAFETY
Safety Escorts

The Department of Campus Safety provides safety escorts for anyone during the evening and early morning hours and for those who are disabled. Any person needing an escort should contact Campus Safety at the dispatch number (949) 214-3000 and state their name and location. For those requesting a ride from their vehicle, please give a description of your vehicle and keep your emergency flashers on until the on-duty officer arrives.

Vehicle Battery Jump-start

Campus Safety and Community Services Officers provide vehicle battery jumps for community members and guests upon request. Please contact Campus Safety Dispatch at (949) 214-3000 and provide a brief description of your vehicle and your location. Remain with your vehicle while waiting for the officer.

Key Assists

On-duty officers perform key assists for faculty, staff, and students. Other rooms on campus may have limited access. Please observe room/lab hours where posted.

Lost and Found

Lost and found is located in Administration 103. After hours, please check with the on-duty officer. If you find a lost item, please turn the item into lost and found.
