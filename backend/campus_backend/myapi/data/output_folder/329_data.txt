Borland-Manske Center Christ College Wing

Dedicated Fall 2019, Concordia University Irvine's newest building - the Borland-Manske Center - brings music and theology together to prepare the next generation of musicians and theologians. The Christ College wing of the Borland-Manske Center houses the Theology library, classrooms, and faculty offices. It is also home to the Mac Lab for Visual Arts - Graphic Design.
