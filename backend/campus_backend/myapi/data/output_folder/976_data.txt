Welcome CCC Alumni!

Our next CCC reunion will be June 9-10, 2023 on the Concordia University Irvine Campus. Activities include a Friday night reception (5 pm) and organ recital (7:30 pm). Saturday includes Chorister rehearsal, Devotions, lunch, plenty of class breakout time, and some friendly competition with board games and card games.

$60 per person (early bird rates end May 9)

Hotel Accomodations

Concordia has favorable rates of $179 plus tax at the Sonesta Irvine, 17941 Von Karman Avenue, Irvine, CA, 92614.

Call 949-863-1999 and mention the CCC Reunion, or Group Code: G6923CUICCC.
Online at https://tinyurl.com/CCCReunion2023
