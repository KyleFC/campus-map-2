Phi Epsilon Kappa
Mission Statement

Welcome to Concordia University's Phi Epsilon Kappa. The purpose of the club is to provide future health care professionals exposure and education in all fields of sports medicine and education. We strive to prepare future physicians, physical therapists, chiropractors, nurses, and athletic trainers with the ability to evaluate and rehabilitate athletic injuries. The students are encouraged to attend workshops and discussions led by sport medicine professionals.

Membership Qualifications

Membership is open to all Concordia University, Irvine students interested in healthcare post-graduation. If you are interested in joining Phi Epsilon Kappa please contact any of the leaders!!
