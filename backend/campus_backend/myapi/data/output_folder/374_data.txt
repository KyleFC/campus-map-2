Concordia Faculty/Staff Meal Card Payment

Please use the form below to add credit to your faculty or staff meal card for dining at Concordia. You will receive a receipt of payment in your email shortly after submission of the form. Payments will be reflected on your Concordia meal card the next business day.

Concordia offers employee discounts for certain payment amounts. Please refer to the table below for those payment amounts and their corresponding discounted rates.

You Pay	You Receive
$40	$40
$70	$75
$100	$115
$125	$155

* Effective July 1, 2024 - the cost per meal will be $6.00.
