Health Clearance Requirements
New Undergraduate Students

Log in @ https://cui.medicatconnect.com and complete the following requirements. Failure to comply by the deadline will result in a Health Hold that may prevent you from enrollment and moving to housing.

Complete Health Form
Enter dates for the following requirements:
MMR #1 (Measles, Mumps & Rubella) vaccine or positive titers
received after 1 year of age
MMR #2 (Measles, Mumps & Rubella) vaccine or positive titers
at least 4 weeks after the first dose
Submit proof by uploading a picture of immunization record that contains the dates entered above

All of these are done online @ https://cui.medicatconnect.com. We do not accept fax or e-mail copies.

Recommended Immunizations
Meningococcal serogroup A/C/Y/W and B
Hepatitis A
Hepatitis B
Varicella (chickenpox)
HPV (human papilloma virus)
Deadline for completion
Fall semester: August 1
Spring semester: December 1
