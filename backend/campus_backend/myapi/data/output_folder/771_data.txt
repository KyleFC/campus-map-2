Parking on CUI Campus

See Concordia University Irvine Parking Regulations.

All vehicles parking in the campus lots need a parking pass or decal. Please obtain the following passes at their respective locations:

Pass or Decal Type	Location
Visitor Pass	Gatehouses 1 and 2
Temporary Parking Pass (more than a day)	Administration 103
Student Decal (one academic year)	Administration 103
Faculty and Staff Decal 	Administration 103

Students, faculty, and staff members will need their driver's license and current vehicle registration when filling out the parking permit application.

Parking Permit Applications 

Please see the Get Started page.

Parking Citation Fines and Appeals

Please observe all parking regulations on campus. All fines must be paid within 21 calendar days of the receipt of the citation. To appeal a parking citation, please go to citationprocessingcenter.com and follow the below directions:

 Provide the number and date of the citation to look up your citation.
 Select the “appeal citation” option on the left side of the screen.
Note: It may take up to five business days for a citation to be processed on citationprocessingcenter.com.
