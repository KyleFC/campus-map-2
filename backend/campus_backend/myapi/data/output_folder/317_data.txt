Guided Tour of Student Union 
The Rodney Lawrence Grimm Memorial Student Union

Students gather daily in the newly renovated caf serviced by Bon Appetit. At Eagles’ Landing, commuters and residents can grab a Starbucks or study at the outdoor lounge. The Student Union also houses the mailroom, Wellness Center, and hosts ASCUI meetings.
