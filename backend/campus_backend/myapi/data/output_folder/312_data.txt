Find Your Calling

Career Services helps you determine and fulfill your career goals. We foster confidence through self-assessment, research, skill development, and exposure to opportunities.

Our 4 Year Career Roadmap will guide you through tangible steps, taking each year in order to become career-ready. You will gain strategies and tools to begin using even now as a student at Concordia, and as you serve in your vocations out in the community.
