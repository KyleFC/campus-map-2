Meet the Coordinators

The Coordinator team is made up of student leaders that plan the events of WOW. Their goal is to ensure a smooth and fun transition for all new students as they join the Golden Eagle Family. These students work diligently to plan WOW, first-year programs and events, and mentoring opportunities for new students.

Meet the Transfer Mentors

All new transfers will be assigned a Transfer Mentor to help them through their first year at Concordia. Transfer Mentors will connect you with key departments and resources, and invite you to events exclusively for transfer students.
