French Hill & Outdoor Activities

French Hill is a trail accessible just behind our residence halls at Turtle Rock Drive. It offers a nice view of the Santa Ana Mountains. The Turtle Rock-French Hill Loop is a 4.1-mile hike and is rated as moderate.

Located in Orange County in Southern California, our campus is nestled within a safe, gated community on a scenic hilltop in Irvine. With access to paved bike paths, walking trails, parks, and beaches, nature is yours to explore.
