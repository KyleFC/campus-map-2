Accelerated Bachelor of Science in Nursing Tuition

At Concordia University Irvine, we are committed to providing our Accelerated BSN students an education of the highest quality, which can lead to decades of a rewarding career. We believe that having high-quality courses, instructors, facilities, and clinical rotations is vital to the success of an accelerated nursing student. You can count on Concordia to provide you with a top-quality nursing education where the ABSN tuition pays off time and time again.

A BSN opens doors. With your BSN in hand, you can begin working as a nurse and earn a comfortable living. You will also find many opportunities for career advancement.

ABSN Nursing Tuition	Summer/Fall '24 and Spring '25
Application Fee (Non-Refundable)	$50
International Application Processing Fee	$150
