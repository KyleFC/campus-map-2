Concordia's Information Nights are a great way to get to know the university. Students will have a chance to hear from the Admission's staff and visit with deans, faculty and alumni. This is a great event to get to know Concordia at your own pace! Mark your calendar for the next Info Night.

ONLINE ONLY
Thursday, March 21, 2024
6:00 p.m. - 7:00 p.m. PST
