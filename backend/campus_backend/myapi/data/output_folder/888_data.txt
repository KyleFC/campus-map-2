Faculty in Residence

Monique Nunes, MA serves as the Director of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion at Concordia University Irvine. She has served in student life and administrative roles at Concordia College New York, Valparaiso University, and Baltimore Lutheran High School. Known for her gifts in sacred music, Monique has performed at hundreds of church events around the world. Monique lives on campus with her husband, John and together they have 6 children and 12 grandchildren.
