Visa and Immigration Information for Current F1 International Students

Protecting your immigration status is one of your most important responsibilities as an international student. The Office of Global Programs is here to help you educate yourself about your rights and responsibilities.

A few of the many areas of immigration we can advise on:

F-1 Immigration Responsibilities
Travel, Re-entry & VISA Renewal
Transferring Immigration Status (To/From American University)
Change of Visa Status
Reinstatement
Withdrawing, Canceling, or Dismissal from Concordia University Irvine
Study Abroad & Permit to Study
Extending your I-20 completion date
Inviting Family Members to the U.S.
Concurrent Enrollment
SSN Information

It is the responsibility of each student to be familiar with the immigration requirements related to your status. Following the guidelines below will help you maintain valid F-1 immigration status. If you have specific questions about your status, you can schedule an appointment to meet with your PDSO.

Keep your passport valid for at least 6 months into the future.
Keep your local address updated.
Maintain full-time enrollment.
Undergraduate - Minimum 12 units/semester
Graduate - Minimum 6 units/semester
Get PDSO approval before a withdrawal from Concordia University Irvine.
Attend the school noted on your I-20.
Keep your I-20 valid at all times.
Get a travel signature.
Request Authorization for Employment;
