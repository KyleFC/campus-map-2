The Partnership

As a Concordia University Irvine Smart Alliance Partner your organization will be eligible for:

Tuition discount (Graduate and OBD Programs)
Waived application fee
Complimentary Evaluation of prior learning for degree articulation

If you would like more information on setting up a partnership with your organization, contact Billy Maxfield (949) 214-3577.
