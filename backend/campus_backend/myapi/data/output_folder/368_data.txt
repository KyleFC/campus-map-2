Introducing the new MyCUI experience
New to the MyCUI Experience?

 Concordia University Irvine is bringing you into the next generation of connectivity to the Concordia community with the new MyCUI experience. Engaging with fellow Eagles (students, faculty, and staff) is now easier than ever. Customize your experience to stay connected on and off campus with your desktop, tablet, or phone.

Digital ID

No need to carry a card around. Use the Digital ID to pay for meals at the cafeteria and Eagle's Landing.

Customize

Personalizing your dashboard with helpful tools, groups, and pages makes MyCUI your own.

Connect

Endless opportunities to connect with Eagles everywhere with just a click or a tap via messaging and activity feeds.

Discover

Identify communities, resources, and services that align with your interests with the global search feature.

Academics

Link directly to your courses in Blackboard and access your academic profile from one convenient location.

Community

Find clubs to join, events to attend, and support for academic success and wellness.

Athletics

Stay up-to-date on live and upcoming Eagle athletic events and results.

Services

Check the menus from Bon Appetit, pay your student bill, and find important registration information.
