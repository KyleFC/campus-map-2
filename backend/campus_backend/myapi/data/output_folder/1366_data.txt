SAMI NEWPORT
Art Major: Studio Art
Graphic Design Minor

What is one thing you have valued the most during your time at Concordia University Irvine so far?

Although there are so many things I value at Concordia, I would say that the access that I have to all of the art supplies/rooms are what I value most.

What do you hope to do with the skills you've gained at Concordia University Irvine after graduation?

I hope to simply share my work with others in a positive and beneficial manner.
