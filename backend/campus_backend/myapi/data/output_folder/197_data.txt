Career Development

Your gifts will strongly influence the direction of your career and your family life. Concordia can help you focus your gifts and apply them to your calling. The Center for Career and Vocation can help you to identify which careers might be a good fit for your particular gifts and talents and provide you with the resources to promote yourself in today's job market.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Students seek counseling for a variety of reasons such as grief and loss, relationship issues, depression, anxiety, eating disorders, controlling usage of alcohol and drugs, life purpose and direction, family conflict, and assistance in making better decisions. No one will be turned away for financial issues.

LEARN MORE
CU Active Fitness Center

Exercise equipment including treadmills, bikes, a rowing machine, free weights, weight machines, and an elliptical machine are free for student use.

LEARN MORE
Paul and Cathy Schroeder Library & Learning Commons

The Schroeder Library at Concordia University Irvine offers access to books, media, research databases, group study rooms and reference help.

 
LEARN MORE
Disability Access Services

Disability Access Services provides assistance to students with physical, medical, psychological and learning disorders and is committed to ensuring that Concordia University Irvine is a campus that is welcoming and inclusive of all its students, faculty, and staff.

LEARN MORE
International Student Support

Concordia's Moon International Center provides support and the tools you will need to succeed as a student at Concordia.

LEARN MORE
Wellness Center

The Wellness Center at Concordia offers FREE office visits to full-time undergraduate students. Part-time and graduate students pay only $25.00 for each medical office visit. The Wellness Center also stocks free samples of decongestants, cough medications, throat lozenges, acetaminophen, ibuprofen, and other medications for common illnesses and injuries.

Writing Studio

Students in all stages of the writing process can benefit from advice and feedback. Concordia's Writing Studio is here to provide that feedback and help you become a better reader and writer of your own work.
