Fourth Year | Job Search & Grad School Planning

As a fourth year student, now is the time to work on action plans for launching into your vocation as a worker or continue your vocation as a student in graduate school. Below are steps to take in your final year of the 4 Year Career Roadmap:

Create a strategic plan with a career coach on how to solidify your personal career brand and market yourself to potential employers.
Learn interview skills and conduct practice interviews with a Career Coach
Attend a Career Fair and/or Grad Fair
Work with the Writing Studio to review personal statements for graduate school
Update Handshake profile and resume to explore full-time employment
Review your resume with a Career Coach
Follow us on social media
Attend workshops + events
