South County Outreach
CONCORDIA serves

South County Outreach serves homeless families, the underemployed, unemployed, seniors, veterans, and other at-risk residents of Orange County. Volunteers will be sorting and serving in their food market, which distributes emergency non-perishable and fresh food daily to as many as 1,500 residents a month.

 Tuesday, September 26, 2023, 9:15am - 12:30pm

Participants will board busses at Grimm Hall at the Departure Time, please arrive 15 minutes prior to your departure.
