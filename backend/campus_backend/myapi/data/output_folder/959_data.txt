Class Registration Survey for New Students

Congratulations and welcome to Concordia University Irvine! The Academic Advising Team is eager to assist you with academic planning and guidance throughout your undergraduate tenure at CUI.

Please note the class registration process does not begin until June for Fall start, and December for Spring start. You will be notified via your Eagles email once your schedule is available.

We are very excited you have decided to join the Eagles family and look forward to working with you in this process!
